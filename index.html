üîç Key Findings

  • Approximately 40% of total revenue remained pending, affecting liquidity
  • Sales generated ~96% of revenue but experienced the highest payment delays
  • Revenue dependency on just 5 customers created concentration risk
  • High-value transactions were associated with longer payment delays
  • Rental generators were significantly underutilized
